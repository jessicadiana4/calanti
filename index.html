CHAIRPERSON'S MESSAGE

LIBRANDO BALTAN
Brgy. Chairman

        It is our great pressure to welcome you to the official website of Barangay Calantipayan, Municipality of Lopez, Province of Quezon.
        I am Librando V. Baltan, the Barangay Chairman, and I say, "Karangalan po naming kayo'y paglingkuran." We are dedicated to serving the people by providing necessary information and sharing the mission and vision of our Barangay.
        To my beloved constituents, as you browse through our homepage, you can access the necessary information and learn about activities that will help you as citizens in this barangay. And to the visitors, It is my pleasure to show you the beauty of our beloved Barangay. The history and photo gallery will give you insights into the way of life here and why people love it. We also warmly welcome all of you to visit personally and experience the place that nature has blessed us with, offering a happy and easy life with basic necessities due to its good geographical location. The farmland, river, and sea make it a strategic location for business as well.
        As a child-friendly Barangay, We believe that children are our future, and we prioritize their needs, as well as those of senior citizens, As the Sangguniang Barangay, we are commited to working as one and providing services to our people. My mission as Barangay Chairman is to give them "Maayos at Epektibong Liderato," which means providing the best service to our constituents by fulfilling their neccesary needs with the help of God.
        Once again, thank you for visiting our official website "Karangalan po naming kayo'y paglingkuran."
